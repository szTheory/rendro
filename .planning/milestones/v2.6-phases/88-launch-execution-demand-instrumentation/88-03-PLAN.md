| T-88-09 | Spoofing | Blocked-document submissions | mitigate | Templates collect source URL, requester context, org/app, and permission/anonymization fields; maintainer applies `adoption:counted` manually. |
| T-88-10 | Tampering | Default labels | mitigate | Create labels before templates and test that `adoption:counted` is not a default. |
| T-88-11 | Information Disclosure | Private fixtures or reports | mitigate | Template requests minimal fixture/repro notes and permission to quote/anonymize rather than confidential uploads. |

</threat_model>
