<objective>
Introduce structured diagnostics to the document pipeline to make non-fatal layout decisions transparent and observable.

Purpose: Instead of failing on overflow or silently pushing content without explanation, the pipeline records decisions (like table splits and forced page breaks) to the document struct. This changeset-like pattern is highly idiomatic and avoids polluting the `Rendro.Telemetry` surface.
Output: A modified `Paginate` stage that aggregates diagnostics and attaches them to `%Rendro.Document{}`.
</objective>
